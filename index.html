Brand and Project Outline

Apollo is a technological brand looking to revolutionise the future through the use of the internet and neurological science. It introduces the idea of being able to access particular webpages with your mind through the use of a neurologically implanted device. Chips containing encrypted information allows the device to access a website on comand, mimicking that of a photographic memory, we call them MindSights.

The device consists of an earpiece that reads brain activity and is able to comunicate with the mind and project images clearly into the eyes of the wearer.

Style & Visual Direction

The design of the website is going to focus on looking professional and consise. This will be acheived through the use of large images with a clean white font over the top and a very sharp general design.

Colour Scheme

The colour scheme for Apollo is limited to three colours, a dark grey, a dark white and a deep blue. This keeps the brand looking professional whilst still being able to draw attention to areas where needed. There is a satisfying contrast between the three colours and they all work well together.

Typography

The fonts used are Minion Pro and Alien League Bold. Minon Pro is used as a base text typeface as it is clean & readable and looks good. Alien League Bold is used as a title typeface that gives a more futeristic vibe as this company looks to take a step into the future.
